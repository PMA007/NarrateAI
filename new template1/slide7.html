Campus Overview

Spread across ~80 acres in an urban setting at Dwarapudi, Vizianagaram, the campus offers a vibrant academic atmosphere blending modern infrastructure with greenery.

Core Academic Infrastructure

Features a Central Library with extensive resources, e-learning classrooms, specialized department laboratories, a digital auditorium, and campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity.

Student Amenities

Comprehensive support facilities including hostels, sports & fitness centers, canteen, banking/ATM services, dispensary, and on-campus staff quarters.
