Our
History

Tracing the evolution of engineering excellence in North Andhra, from a constituent college to an autonomous university named in honor of the literary giant Gurajada Apparao.

2007: Foundation

Established as the University College of Engineering, Vizianagaram, functioning as a constituent college of JNTU Hyderabad.

2008: Transition

Following university trifurcation, the institution became a constituent college of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada.

2021: Legislation

The Government of Andhra Pradesh introduced a bill in the Legislative Assembly to upgrade the college into an autonomous university.

2022: Establishment

Officially established as JNTU-GV on January 12th via the JNTU (Amendment) Act, 2021, serving the North Andhra region.
