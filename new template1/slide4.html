JNTU-GV offers a comprehensive range of engineering and technological programs designed to foster innovation and academic excellence.
Course Offerings

Undergraduate (B.Tech): Seven distinct engineering streams including CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, IT, and Metallurgical Engineering.

Postgraduate (M.Tech): Eight specialized programs offering advanced research opportunities and technical depth.

Scope: A robust ecosystem covering Engineering, Pharmacy, and Management disciplines.

Admission & Support

Admissions: Merit-based entry via AP EAPCET for undergraduate aspirants and GATE/PGECET for postgraduate candidates.

Support Systems: Dedicated directorates for Academic Planning, R&D, IQAC, and Industrial Relations & Placements ensure holistic student development.
